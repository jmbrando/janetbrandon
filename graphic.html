How to Make Dutch Pancakes

Creator(s): Janet Brandon

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator

As an instructional design project, this recipe for Dutch pancakes was designed to show clarity and simplicity in a set of instructions and to deliver a delicious recipe that could be used by even the newest of cooks. Each element of this template was designed with care and following a theme that alludes back to the Dutch colours of both its national colour and national flag.
